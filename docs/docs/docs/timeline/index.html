    Timeline

    Explore a timeline highlighting key events in the fight for Civil Rights from Baltimore, Maryland, and the nation. View the timeline full-screen.

    We created this timeline using TimelineJS and Google Sheets. You can make your own timeline based on the timeline above. Make a copy our sheet, add or remove events, and then use TimelineJS to create an custom timeline for your personal website, classroom, or community group.

    Please help us make this timeline better! Make a comment on the open Google Sheet we use to create the interactive timeline above. Helpful comments including suggesting events that we are missing or correcting any inaccurate information. You can also contact us through our website or send us an email at info@baltimoreheritage.org.
